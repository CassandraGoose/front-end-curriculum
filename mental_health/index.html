Additional Resources Mental Health Resources

Turing values the mental health of all of it students. Students in Colorado can schedule time with Jun, our school counselor, on-site depending on his availability, listed here.

Jun lists his availability the week before. Join the slack channel #health-wellness for more information from Jun, as well as community wellness tips, reminders, and events.

If you feel that you need additional resources, please consider the following.

  • For immediate crisis intervention: Colorado Crisis Services
  • For tools to use to support ongoing efforts review the resources available here
