Your mental health is important! We know Turing is stressful. Here are several resources that are available to you!

If you feel comfortable reaching out to your instructors, we're always happy to listen, and we can refer you to other staff members who can assist you if you need further help than we can give.

Students in Colorado can schedule time with Jun, our school counselor, on-site depending on his availability, listed here.

Jun lists his availability the week before. Join the slack channel #health-wellness for more information from Jun, as well as community wellness tips, reminders, and events.

For financial concerns, you can reach out directly to Darren Smith on Slack; he can connect you with various services (especially in the Colorado area), or help you find services in your area, to help alleviate financial stressors.

If you need additional resources, please consider the following.

  • For immediate crisis intervention in Colorado: Colorado Crisis Services
  • List of national crisis intervention hotlines: Mental Health America Immediate Response Resources
  • For tools to use to support ongoing efforts review the resources available here
